Title: Shooter had lengthy criminal history including weapons charges, police say | CNN
Summary: A woman opened fire at Joel Osteen's megachurch, injuring a child and another man. She was shot and killed by off-duty officers. The shooter, Genesse Ivonne Moreno, had a history of criminal charges and mental health problems. Witnesses described hearing erratic gunfire. Authorities found no explosives despite her claiming to have a bomb.
