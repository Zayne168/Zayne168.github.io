Title: Illinois stabbing spree suspect charged with murder after rampage leaves four dead, seven injured | Fox News
Summary: 22-year-old Christian Soto charged with four counts of first-degree murder in a stabbing spree in Rockford, Illinois. Four dead, seven injured. Soto claimed drugs made him stab victims. Victims include a 15-year-old girl and a 25-year veteran postal worker. Three injured still hospitalized. Soto to appear in court to determine jail status.
